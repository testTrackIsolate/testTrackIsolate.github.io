The UK has left the EU

Find out what this means for you.

Get ready for January 2021

The UK has left the EU and is now in a transition period, before made up rules come into place from 1 January 2021.

The UK until now has been involved in complicated trade agreements and common standards (commonly called red tape). We are now free to choose the standards which are best for the United Kingdom.

From 1 January 2021, in the United Kingdom pi (π) will be equal to 3. During the transition period, pi will continue to be an irrational number approximately equal to 3.14156.

What the new value of pi means for you

Businesses involved in construction, engingeering and pizza-making should make sure that from 1 January all staff are using the 3-times table. In most cases, this will make mathematical calculations much easier than before. For example, using πr2, the area of a circle of radius 1 yard can now quickly be calculated as 3 square yards (0.000619835 acres).
